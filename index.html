About me

Engineer in Data Science, I am now a PhD Student specialized in Machine Learning and Time Series processing.

My work aims to develop a method to learn the global model of functionning of a system from its logs or sensor data using Timed Automata.

My PhD takes place in the LACODAM team in the Inria lab of Rennes.

You can contact me by email or via Linkedin .

Publications

Passive Learning of Timed Automata from Logs (Student Abstract)

Lénaïg Cornanguer, 2021
AAAI-21 Finalist of the Three-Minute Presentation Contest (video).
We propose a novel algorithm to passively learn deterministic Timed Automata from events sequences associated with the delay occurring between them. This algorithm produces models that are more specific than State-of-the-Art algorithms and that has a better identification of the temporal constraints applying on the systems.
