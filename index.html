About Me

PhD in Computer Science, I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at CISPA in the EDA team, working on causality discovery in time series.
My research mainly focus on exploring observational data, often with a temporal dimension, to extract models of behavior, temporal logic, and causal relationships.
